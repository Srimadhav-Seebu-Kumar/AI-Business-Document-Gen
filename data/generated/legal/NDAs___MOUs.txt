1. Purpose

2. Confidential Information
"Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, whether orally or in writing, that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.

3. Non-Disclosure
The Receiving Party agrees not to disclose or use any Confidential Information for any purpose other than the Purpose, without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party. The Receiving Party shall take all reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality of the Confidential Information.
