Customer Journey Map

1) Awareness:
- Potential customers become aware of the platform through online advertisements, social media promotions, and word-of-mouth recommendations.
- They are intrigued by the concept of personalized and up-to-date travel guides and maps for various destinations.

2) Consideration:
- Customers visit the platform's website or download the mobile app to explore its features and offerings.
- They read reviews and testimonials from other users to gauge the platform's reliability and usefulness.
- Customers compare the platform with other similar services to determine its unique value proposition.

3) Decision:
- Customers decide to sign up for a free account to access basic features and content on the platform.
- They may upgrade to a premium subscription to unlock exclusive travel guides and personalized recommendations.
- Customers may also make in-app purchases for additional content or experiences.

4) Purchase:
- Customers make bookings for accommodations, transportation, and activities through the platform's integrated booking platforms.
- They may also click on advertisements or affiliate links to support the platform's revenue model.
- Customers receive confirmation emails and notifications for their bookings and purchases.

5) Experience:
- Customers use the platform's detailed travel guides and interactive maps to plan their trips effectively.
- They explore attractions, restaurants, and accommodations based on personalized recommendations and user reviews.
- Customers share their experiences and feedback on the platform to help improve its offerings for future users.

6) Loyalty:
- Satisfied customers continue to use the platform for their future travel needs and recommend it to friends and family.
- They may leave positive reviews and ratings on app stores and review websites to increase the platform's visibility.
- Customers may also participate in loyalty programs or referral schemes to earn rewards for their continued support.
